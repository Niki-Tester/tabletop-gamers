About Us

Here at TableTop Gamers we are dedicated to you having fun.

We host a venue where you can play games, and socialize with a diverse group of people, where we all love playing table-top games.

When we opened our store, back in 2017 we become the one stop shop to buy table-top games in the area, this was all because of our huge collection of games from all around the world.

But That Wasn't Enough!
We wanted to create a community where like minded people could have fun. So we created a space above our store, where anyone can come along, be social and play games, be that just for some fun, or for special occasions.

Where we play!

TableTop Gamers has dedicated spaces that are available for booking. This could be for Team Building Exercises, Charity Events, Competitions, Parties & Celebrations and more
